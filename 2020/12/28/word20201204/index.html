單字學習 20201204

  1. sweep (n)大獲全勝

They won this game in a clean sweep.

  1. approach (n) 方法
    There are many approaches to solve this problem.

  2. oath(n)誓言
    He took an oath to give up drinking (alcohol 可以不用).

  3. ordinance(n)(限制性)法條 條例
    Under the ordinance, you should mask up in public.

  4. muffle (v) 包裹住 摀住聲音……聲音變小
    She was muffled up in a huge blanket.

  5. wax (n) 蠟
    This insect produces a lot of wax to protect itself.

  6. rattle (v) 發出咯咯聲
    The door rattle when someone opening it.
    He got rattled when he was fired. 情緒上的緊張

  7. predator(n)掠奪者 捕食者 (人)犯罪上的掠奪者
    Animals protect their young from predators by covering them.

  8. purgatory (n) 煉獄
    Exercise is purgatory for him.

  9. stuff(n/v)東西 物品／thing
    I am not available this afternoon. I’ve got stuff to do。

  10. chunk(n) 木頭
    The desk is made by a chunk of wood.
    a chunk of something 一部分的 不完整

  11. prodigious (a) 強大 巨大
    This invention benefits people a prodigious amount.

  12. stimulus (n) 刺激 刺激物, 鼓勵者
    Think of/ Take every failure as a stimulus for your own progress.
    將每一次失敗都視為對自己進步的刺激。

  13. orbit (n) 軌道 軌跡 、形容人的影響力 (v)沿軌道圍繞（行星或恆星）運行
    The earth moves in an elliptical orbit around the sun.
    地球繞太陽以橢圓型的軌道運行
    within the orbit of something

  14. playful (a) 調皮 好玩的
    After his performance, my playful mood immediately returned.
    經過他的表演，好玩的心情立刻又回來了。

  15. disorganized (a) 雜亂無章的
    This restaurant is so disorganized that there is no menu and no waiters know what the dishes are.
    這家餐廳雜亂無章，因為沒有菜單，沒有服務員知道什麼菜。

  16. float (v) 浮動 (n) 彩車 花車
    There is a corpse floating on the sea.
    在海面上漂浮著一具屍體。
    carnival float 嘉年華花車
