單字學習 20201217

  1. intimate (a) 親密的

    His family have a intimate connection with each other.

  2. steady (a/v) 穩步的 持續的
    Steady performance is my standard for choosing players to join my team.

  3. rub (v) 按摩 摩擦
    This spa provides back rub service.
    rub off 感染
    His optimism rubbed off on me.
    rub sth. on someone’s face
    rub off some dirt from my face 一些泥土從我臉上弄掉
    rub somebody up the wrong way 好事在某些時候不是好事
    His eagerness rubbed me up in the wrong way.
    rub shoulders with someone 炫耀

  4. rattle off 不假思索講出
    She could probably rattle off all the phone numbers in our company.

  5. reception (n) 接待會 接待處
    You can meet a lot of movie star at the reception tomorrow.
    poor reception 手機訊號不好

  6. spite (v/n) 傷害 / 儘管 刁難 嫉妒 傷害人
    In spite of the rain,a lot of people gathered in the shop.
    out of spite 只是為了傷害人
    He pushed over her chair out of spite/to spite us.
    pure/sheer spite 只是為了傷害人

  7. affirmation (n) 肯定 同意 批准 (confirm)
    She got the affirmation of the approval of the project from her boss.
    affirmative
    affirm (正式的) 確認

  8. trite (a) 無聊 不誠懇 平庸 過時
    The songs you listen to are so trite.

  9. errand (n) 差事 跑腿
    He got some errands to run in the city hall.
    city hall 市政府
    (send someone on) a fool errand

  10. wise (a) 明智
    He can be only wise after the event.
    be none the wiser 依然不明白

  11. brilliant (a) 輝煌 光明的 出色的
    Everyone hopes they have a brilliant career.
    每個人都希望他們擁有輝煌的職業生涯。

  12. reconsolidation (n) 重新整合 重新合併
    reconsolidation of common products can create new business opportunities.
    重新整合普通產品可以創造出新的商機

  13. Kimchi (n) 泡菜
    Kimchi dishes are Korean national cuisines.

  14. waiver (n) 免支付聲明；棄權聲明書
    Disabled students can apply for some government fee waiver.
    殘疾學生可以申請一些政府費用減免。

  15. defense (n/v) 防禦
    In my defense, I thought he tried to hurt me.
    在我的防禦中，我認為他試圖傷害我
    In someone defense 以某人的立場
