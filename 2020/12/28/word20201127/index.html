  1. explicitly (adv) 明確地

  2. craft 工藝 技術
    She crafts her dress for the birthday party.
  3. celestial (adj) 神聖的 天上的
    She is as beautiful as a celestial being.
  4. coop (v) 關 (n) 籠子
    Princess was crooped up in the castle.
  5. bliss out 興喜若狂…high
    He was blissed-out from the surprise from his parent.
  6. prisoner (n) 犯人
    His task is to more the prisoners to another girl.
    幾種犯人用法：
    被關的人 prisoner
    關在某個機構 inmate
    被定罪的人 convict
  7. rip up 撕碎
    He can rip the clothes up easily.
  8. approach (v) 靠近 接近 (n) 方法
    I use the chicken thigh/drumstick to lure the dog approaching me.
    lure引誘
    chicken wings 雞翅
  9. sweep (v) 打掃 掃過 掠過
    Her mom swept off all garbage on the desk.
    Her mom swept the floor.
    sweep sth. under the rug 藏起來某件事（負面）
    sweep someone off their feet 讓那個人跟你陷入愛河
  10. metabolic (adj) 新陳代謝
    Exercise can increase metabolic rate.
  11. border (n) 邊界 邊緣
    The cup is almost on the edge the border of the table.
    The village is on the border of this state.
    country that border China 與中國接壤的國家
  12. rig (v) 操縱
    The bookie主頭 rigged the game to win the stacks.
  13. tweet (v) 發推文 鳴叫
    You should double-check you message/writting before tweeting , because it will be instantly visible to those who follow you.
  14. spare (v) 分出 騰出（禮貌）
    Could you spare some time for me on Sunday？
  15. rhetoric (n) 花言巧語
    I was deeply impressed by the rhetoric in the speech from the little girl.
    這個小女孩在演講中的誇誇其談給我留下了深刻的印象
  16. interpersonal (adj) 人際交往的
    Interpersonal/social/diplomatic skills will help you succeed in your position.
    人際交往能力將幫助您在職位上取得成功。
  17. infringement (n) 侵權
    It is copyright infringement to make a copy of the textbook without authorization.
    私自翻印原文書是侵犯版權的
  18. receptor (n) 受體 感覺器官 話筒 接收器
    受體是一種蛋白質分子，通常存在酵素分子的表面，可以和神經傳導物質（neurotransmitter）、荷爾蒙（hormone）、藥物或是毒物等配體（ligand）結合。神經末段
    Humans have more than five millions olfactory receptors.
    人類擁有超過五百萬種嗅覺受體。
  19. piracy (n) 侵犯版權
    (v/n) pirate 海盜 剽竊
    video piracy platform
    Taiwan’s largest video piracy platform 8maple.ru was sued for violation of the copyright law.
    台灣最大的視頻盜版平台楓林網,以違反版權法為由提起訴訟。
  20. voucher (n) 收據 票券 保證人
    This store accepts consumption vouchers.
    invoice 發票（商業交易）
    receipt 收據，憑據
    voucher 收據，票券，禮券
