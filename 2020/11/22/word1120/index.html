單字學習 20201120

  1. context 語境（上下文） 景況 前後關係

see sth. in the context of sth. 在……背景下
ex: In the context of/Because of the covid-19 pandemic, everyone dares not to go to indoor public places.
在covid-19大流行的背景下，每個人都不敢去室內公共場所。
ex: In what context did Tom shout at you?
shout at 不用解釋對你說了什麼
shout to sb 前面會先解釋對你說了什麼
‘Fuck’ He shouted to you.
ex: In what context did he shout at you.
ex: His life and work must be seen in the context of his youth.

  1. tweet (v) 發推文 鳴叫
    ex: I always double-check content before tweeting the post, because it will be instantly visible to those who are following you.

  2. spare (v) 給予（某人足夠的東西）給（某人）；願意付出
    Can you spare some time for me on Sunday?

  3. rhetoric (n) 修辭 花言巧語
    ex: Too much rhetoric sometimes puts the cart before the horse. put the cart before the horse 本末倒置

  4. allegation (n) 指控 斷言
    ex: I have received a letter accusing me of littering亂丟垃圾, and I wish to refute the allegation. 我想反駁這一指控

  5. pissed off 被惹毛了 滾開 感嘆句（不相信）
    ex: I am pissed off that he broke my facial care product.
    annoyed 有點煩的生氣
    irritated 被激怒的
    angry 暴怒
    mad 暴怒
    irated 暴怒

  6. primitive (adj) 原始 基本狀態
    ex: Tom is a primitive person because he still lives in the cave.
    ex: Hunger is a primitive desire of human.

  7. sanitizer (n) 消毒劑 v sanitize
    They use some sanitizer to clear their hands before dinner.

  8. deliver (v) 交付 傳遞 n delivery on the front desk

  9. squirt (n/v) 注射 容器
    She needs a squirt bottle for 75％ alcohol sanitizer.

  10. craft (n) 手工藝 (v)手工製作
    The pottery陶藝 craft from Yingge is the best.

  11. hypocrisy (n) 虛偽 偽善
    People think this policy from the government is hypocrisy.

  12. aggressive (adj)對手的
    Mimics firing a gun with hands to the opponent is aggressive.

  13. explicitly (adv) 明確地 明白地 露骨
    explicit (adj)露骨(18禁)

  14. narrator (n) 解說員
    Tom became an audio book narrator.

  15. persecute (v) 迫害
    Colonizer殖民者 persecute the local religion.

  16. faux pas 失言 失禮
    Listening to music in the public with an earphone is a faux pas.
    Pardon my French.
    過去意思：有階級關係會這樣
    現在意思：請原諒我罵髒話。
