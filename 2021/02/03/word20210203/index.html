單字學習 20210203

  1. allergic (a) 過敏性的

If your lips have an allergic reaction to lipstick, you can use Vaseline before going to bed to enhance moisture.

  1. bear sb/sth out 為…作證，證明，證實
    Whether he was involved in the killing has not been borne out yet.
    他是否參與謀殺尚未得到證實。

  2. refrain (n) 副歌
    The refrain of “Little Apple” is brainwashed and catchy. 朗朗上口
    (v)避免
    In order to refrain from saying the wrong thing, practice speaking more often on weekdays.

  3. revenge (v) 報復；報仇
    Control your own revenge shopping when you are in a bad mood.
    心情不好時要控制住自己的報復幸消費

  4. wrong (v)委屈；不公正地對待；冤枉
    He felt deeply wronged by being accused of stealing.

  5. institute (n) 研究所 學院
    He graduated from a famous foreign institute of technology.

  6. metropolitan (n) 大都市
    Housing prices in metropolitans are usually quite expensive.

  7. understatement (n) 輕描淡寫
    The content of the product manual is an understatement, so users have to google how to use it.

  8. sightseeing (n) 觀光 遊覽
    We took a sightseeing bus to tour Taipei.

  9. breach (v) 違反 破壞 缺口
    A car turning right at a red light breaches traffic laws.

  10. propaganda (n) 宣傳
    Proper propaganda of our products can make them sell better.
    適度的宣傳我們的產品可以讓產品賣的更好。

  11. petrochemical (n) 石化製品, 石化工業
    He has 3 years of petrochemical work experience.

  12. institutional (a) 制度的 機構的
    This seems to be an institutional problem, not just his own problem.
    這似乎是一個體制問題，而不僅僅是他自己的問題。

  13. roll out 推出（新産品、服務等）；實行（新制度）
    Last month their company rolled out the latest 5G technology.

  14. immune (a) 免疫的 不受影響的 豁免的
    If you want to be immune to the virus, you can exercise more.

  15. shrink (v) 縮小 退縮
    Whenever he encounters a situation where he must speak English, he always shrinks and hides.
    每當他遇到必須說英語的情況時，他總是會縮手縮腳。

  16. rampant (a)（不好的事情）猖獗的，泛濫的
    Crime was rampant in this country.
    這個國家犯罪很昌盛

  17. kick off （足球比賽）開球，開始
    The football match will kick off in the afternoon.

  18. contraband (n)違禁品
    E-cigarettes are considered contraband in Taiwan.
    電子煙在台灣被視為違禁品。

  19. labor (n) 勞動 （labour的美式拼寫）
    The highest cost of opening a company is the labor cost.
